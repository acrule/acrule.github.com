Hello. My name is Adam Rule. I am a PhD student in Cognitve Science at UC San Diego where I work with Jim Hollan in the Design Lab. I study human-computer interaction, developing technologies that support complex and fragmented work. I get particularly excited about interruptions, information visualization, and clinical informatics.
